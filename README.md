# Prism - Mental Health & Psychologist Booking App

## Description:
Prism is a mobile application designed to help users improve their mental well-being by accessing articles written by professional psychologists and booking appointments with them. Users can interact with content, view notifications, and manage their profile easily.

The app allows for seamless registration and login using a phone number and password. Users can explore a variety of articles, leave comments, and engage with other users through likes. In addition, users can book consultations with psychologists, view their post history, and keep track of notifications related to their activities within the app.

All content in Prism is carefully curated, compiled, and deployed before being made available on the platform.

Prism is available for both iOS and Android devices.

**Main Features Include:**
- Register and log in using phone number and password.
- Browse articles written by psychologists and interact through likes and comments.
- Book appointments with psychologists.
- View post history and notifications.
- Manage user profiles with options to update profile details, change avatar, and reset password.
- All content is moderated before going live on the app.
